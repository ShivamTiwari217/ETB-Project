Glossary of Tactical Terms

Half-Space

The vertical channel between the touchline and the center of the pitch. Often considered the most tactically valuable zone for exploitation.

xG (Expected Goals)

A metric quantifying the quality of a scoring opportunity based on historical data. It ranges from $0$ (impossible) to $1$ (certain goal).
